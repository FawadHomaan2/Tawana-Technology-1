About Us

Empowering Through Technology

Tawana Technology (توانا تکنالوژی) is a leading educational center and IT services provider based in Kabul, Afghanistan. Known for its focus on Cybersecurity and high-tech vocational training, it operates under the Afghanistan Cyber Defence Intelligence (ACDI) initiative.

The center offers a variety of professional and practical IT courses designed to prepare students for the global job market. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, Tawana Technology is shaping the future of IT education in Afghanistan.

Using modern teaching methodologies and hands-on training, we make learning engaging and effective, preparing our students for real-world challenges in the technology sector.

Our Programs

"Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today." At Tawana Technology, we offer comprehensive training programs in Cybersecurity, Networking, Web Development, and Digital Literacy. Our mission is to empower individuals with practical skills for the global job market.
